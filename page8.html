Gateway features

The device is composed by two parts: one reserved for wireless and internet connection, the other for video recording. Each gateway supports 200 wireless devices and 4 IP cameras.
There is a built-in battery in case of blackout, that provides full access to all the functions, the system will also notify the owner in case of power cut or if any of the sensors became unavailable.
