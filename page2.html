Security accessories

VIAS is also a complete system for Video monitoring. You will be able to connect IP cameras, watch the live video, get alert notifications, and record 24h videos. Live video and recordings are (as the rest of the system) accessible both locally and remotely.
Video recording can be triggered by the security sensors connected to the gateway, your alarm system will be less affected by false alerts.

24/7 recording

The VIAS system let you record the videos from your cameras 24/7.

ONVIF

The system supports ONVIF, so you are able to install all your favorite cameras.

Quick access

You can access all your camera from the smartphone App, and share access to some cameras to some users.
