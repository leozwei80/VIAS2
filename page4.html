VIAS App

VIAS smartphone app let you control your system from everywhere. You can turn ON/OFF automation devices, receive alert notifications, create schedule events, watch your IP cameras live and play videos recorded in the NVR. 

Total security

Watch the video from your cameras, receive alert notifications, check the list of access directly from your smartphone.

Customizable

Sensors are divided into gateways, partitions & zones. Each one with different properties. Add your favorites sensors to a special section for quick access

Share to others

Create multiple users with different access rights: choose which partitions they can access, the time they can access, or block a user if needed.























Sharing

Sharing is also easy, just create a separate partition with all the sensors you need to share and create a new user with password access. It is also possible to create users without App access but with a time limit keypad code access.

Energy consumption

You can check the graphic of energy consumption to know how much you are consuming and when. You can also make sure if some electronics are consuming too much and need to be replaced.
